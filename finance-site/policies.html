Purpose

Ensure prudent deployment of capital for low-value assets while maintaining operational agility and proper financial controls for Guardianwing's drone monitoring and safety operations.

Scope

This policy applies to all equipment, software, and tooling purchases ‚â§ $10,000 (before tax) funded by Guardianwing LLC. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Drone hardware and accessories

Procurement Process

  1. Requisition: Requester completes purchase requisition form with detailed specifications, cost justification, and preferred vendor information.
  2. Documentation: Attach required documentation per approval matrix above.
  3. Approval: Obtain electronic sign-off via the finance portal workflow system.
  4. Purchase Order: Finance team issues official Purchase Order to vendor.
  5. Asset Tracking: Finance records asset tag and adds to company inventory system.
  6. Receipt & Payment: Verify receipt of goods/services and process payment within payment terms.

Required Documentation

For All Purchases:

  • Business justification and expected use case
  • Budget code and cost center allocation
  • Vendor information and payment terms

For Purchases $2,501-$5,000:

  • At least 2 vendor quotes for comparison
  • Technical specifications document

For Purchases $5,001-$10,000:

  • ROI analysis with payback period
  • Impact on operational efficiency
  • Alternative solution evaluation

Emergency Exceptions

Safety-critical items or urgent operational needs may bypass standard thresholds with:

  • Joint approval from CEO & CFO
  • Written justification for emergency status
  • Retroactive completion of standard documentation within 5 business days
  • Review at next executive team meeting

Vendor Management

All vendors must be approved through our vendor onboarding process including:

  • W-9 form completion
  • Insurance certificate verification
  • Payment terms negotiation
  • Master Service Agreement (for recurring vendors)

Compliance & Audit

This policy is subject to:

  • Quarterly review by Finance and Operations teams
  • Annual audit by external accounting firm
  • Monthly reporting to board of directors
  • Immediate update for regulatory changes affecting drone operations
